My Experience

My first week at AkiraChix was great.First of all, I did not expect to find so many nationalities which greatly shocked me;I was very thrilled to have an Ethiopian and a Rwandese roommate;it has given me a chance to learn about the different cultures and beliefs that everyone holds dear to heart.I love that the Akirachix team greatly cares for all our needs and aims to make sure we are all okay at all capacities without discrimination.My other biggest fascination is about the food that we're served.I did not expect us to be served by professional chefs everyday, and I love the fact that we don't have a specific menu for the meals which makes us always look forward to what's next for lunch or supper.I also love how we have our individual laptops to work on and how flexible the classes are.

Exciting moments at Akirachix

  1. Fire safety training session
  2. Show and tells where we get to present our works both individual and groupworks
  3. Groupworks where we get to interact with different great minds
  4. Games such as blocks, foosball
  5. The 4 O'clock tea which is always accompanied by delicious snacks
